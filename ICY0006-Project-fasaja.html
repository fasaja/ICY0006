New Car Sales in Norway by Make¶

In this project, the monthly car sales in Norway by make from 2007 to 2017 are examined. The public dataset used in this project is used from www.kaggle.com website and the exact link to the dataset is https://www.kaggle.com/dmi3kno/newcarsalesnorway There are five different variables in this dataset as Year (year of sales month/numerical), Month (month of sales make/numerical), Make (car make such as Mazda/categorical), Quantity (number of cars sold/numerical) and Pct (percent share in monthly total/numerical).

Stage 4¶

Swedish Lottery The Lotto is by far the most popular game of chance in Sweden. All Swedish Lotto Bets, by default, are for two separate draws, Lotto 1 and Lotto 2, giving you two different chances to win. It costs £0.50 for the Swedish Lotto 1 at or £1 to go for both Lotto 1 and Lotto 2. There are 6 prize tiers for the Swedish Lotto: Matched Numbers Odds of Winning 7 1:6,724,520 6+1 1:240,161 6 1:40,027 5 1:847 4 1:59 3 1:55

Swedish Lotto has no minimum jackpot as prizes are based on official ticket sales. The biggest Drömvinsten(Dream Win) of all time was 237, 697, 528 kr or about £19 million.
